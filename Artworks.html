Artworks

My paintings are a tribute to my family, the traditional land owners in the Central Australian desert. My paintings are an aerial view of the land and represent desert habitats. To create my paintings I use my own experiences and Dreamtime stories in the style by which was taught to me by my elders using ancient iconography in both ochre and contemporary colours.

The Bronwyn Raven Purrula 2026 collection are works that have been produced over the past few years. Fine details and layers of the most popular designs rich in contemporary colours.
